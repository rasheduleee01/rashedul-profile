About Me

I am a proud member of the Institution of Diploma Engineers, Bangladesh (IDEB). I completed my Diploma in Electrical Technology from Dhaka Polytechnic Institute and began my professional career with Bashundhara Industrial Complex Limited, where I was actively involved in large-scale project erection, commissioning, and subsequent plant operations.

Alongside my professional responsibilities, I pursued and successfully completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Hamdard University Bangladesh. This academic journey strengthened my expertise in power systems, control engineering, and industrial automation, enabling me to bridge theoretical knowledge with practical industrial applications.

I have experience in manpower coordination, production planning, and real-time operational reporting using digital communication tools. I am passionate about using modern technology to support industrial development and national progress, and I am always eager to learn and improve my skills. With strong technical, analytical, and leadership abilities, I aim to advance my expertise through higher studies and engineering research, contributing to sustainable industrial growth.
