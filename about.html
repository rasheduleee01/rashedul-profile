About Me

I am a proud member of the Institution of Diploma Engineers, Bangladesh (IDEB). I completed my Diploma in Electrical Technology from Dhaka Polytechnic Institute and earned my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Hamdard University Bangladesh.

I have practical experience in electrical project erection and commissioning. Currently, I am working in cement plant operations using SCADA systems.

I am passionate about using modern technology to support industrial development and national progress. I am always eager to learn more and grow my skills through new experiences.
